Spoke Darts for High-Dimensional Blue Noise Sampling

Scott A. Mitchell, Mohamed S. Ebeida, Muhammad A. Awad, Chonhyon Park, Anjul Patney, Ahmad A. Rushdi, Laura P. Swiler, Dinesh Manocha, Li-Yi Wei

ToG 2018

Summary

We replace point-sampling with radial line-sampling, in Bridson's annular advancing-front algorithm. The output distribution is better theoretically and practically. Applications include Delaunay graphs, global optimization, and robotic motion planning.
